Strategy Recap

Your three detective tools from this week — keep them in mind for the sweep and the test.

Base + suffix

Find the base word, add the suffix: danger → dangerous, free → freedom, surviv(e) → survival.

Remember: some bases change — survive drops the e.

Soft c & g

c and g go soft before e, i, y: refugee, courageous, asylum’s soft sounds.

Remember: keep the e in courageous to hold the soft g.

Tricky & silent bits

Watch the traps: the double-e in refugee, the sy in asylum, the schwa in desperate.

Remember: say tricky words slowly, sound out every part.
